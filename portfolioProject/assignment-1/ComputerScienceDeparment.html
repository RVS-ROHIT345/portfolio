ABOUT US

DEPARMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

Welcome to the Computer Science Department at George Mason University, the largest and fastest-growing department in the College of Engineering and Computing. With nearly 80 full-time faculty members, we offer diverse programs, including two Bachelor of Science degrees, four Master's programs, and a Ph.D. in Computer Science, serving over 3,000 students. Our faculty are leaders in research and education, contributing to interdisciplinary projects and earning numerous awards for their work. Committed to inclusivity and "Computing for All," we foster strong industry ties and support efforts to increase representation of women and minorities in computing. Explore our website to learn more about our programs, research opportunities, and community.
